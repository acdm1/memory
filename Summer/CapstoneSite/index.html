A Sonic Dérive of 7 Puget Sound Parks

Utalizing this symbol ❖ from the main menu, you will be able to return to this position at the top of the attached pages.

On all pages these symbols ☁ and 🧭, will pop-out external pages.
The first for the soundscapes as they appear as a collection (☁), and another containing the full interactive sound-map (🧭)!

This initial page introduces a discussion about the culture to be found around 7 different parks as they appear within their respective boundaries of ambiance around the cities of Tacoma, Steilacoom, and University Place in Washington State.

This soundscape collection project works to accompany a document descriping the application of psychogeography to cartographic field-work under the umbrella of urban studies, and throught the exercise of collecting soundscapes.
